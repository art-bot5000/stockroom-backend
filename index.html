Connect Google Drive

STOCKROOM uses Google Drive to keep your data safe and in sync across devices. Here's exactly what access is needed and why.

📄
Create and edit one file
STOCKROOM creates a single file called stockroom_data.json in your Drive. It can only see files it created — not your other Drive contents.
🔒
No access to your other files
The permission requested is drive.file — the most restricted Drive scope. Google only allows access to files this app created itself.
📧
Why it says "art-bot5000.deno.net"
Google shows the server that handles the secure sign-in step. This is the STOCKROOM backend service — it exchanges your sign-in for a token and immediately sends you back to the app. It does not store your Google password or personal details.
🗑️
You can revoke access any time
Go to myaccount.google.com/permissions to remove STOCKROOM's access at any time. Your Drive file stays — only the permission is revoked.
Your data stays yours. STOCKROOM is open source, stores your items in your own Drive account, and does not sell or share your data.
